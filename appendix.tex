\chapter{Appendix}

The appendix is included as a separate document to limit the size of the thesis. This document contains complete latent space representations of the real-life networks, including additional results on other network segments.

The animations are rasterized to reduce file size, in addition to being more CPU-friendly. This does effect the quality of the visualizations, but should ultimately be sufficient to view the emerging patterns.
